Department Info Course Overview

World Languages

Sep 8th, 2011

Department Chair: Carol McMichael

The World Language Department at Jack M. Barrack Hebrew Academy offers a selection of four world languages to the student body: French, Latin, Spanish, and Arabic (offered only as an Exploratory elective in 2011-2012). In each class, key skills such as understanding, speaking, along with reading and writing, are presented in a way in which students can readily progress and be successful both in the classroom and in the real world which awaits. Our school guarantees the study of a world language for four years. Students who complete the level four are not just well versed in the four skills, but also have been exposed to a quantity of related culture - especially art, music and history.

The study of a world language does require lots of practice at home by the student in order to acquire sufficient competency in the skills. Students are usually assigned a reasonable amount of homework nearly every night which serves to review and to reinforce classroom activities.
