Future Home of Ethical Passages

A special school-wide project involving the study of a special selection of ethical and moral sayings from rabbinic literature called "Pirke Avot,".  PDF and audio files will be posted for both students and parents.

Assessment Policy

Classroom Guidelines

Due Dec 12, 2012: Quiz- Gemara Key Terms from B"M 9 and 10

Due Dec 13, 2012: HW- Intro to Tehillim and questions

Read the JPS Intro to Tehillim (handed out in class today) and answer the questions on side 1 of the worksheet.  

Due Jan 07, 2013: Quiz (oral) - Kiddushin 29a

Due Jan 08, 2013: HW- Questions 1-4 on HO for Psalm 27

Due Jan 17, 2013: Presentations - Tehillim

Due Jan 23, 2013: HW- Rashi on "ha lan v'ha l'hu"

Read, translate and summarize Rashi on the words הא לן והא להו on daf 29b. As always, your work product should be yours alone.

Due Jan 28, 2013: HW- EJ Article and questions on Megillat Esther

Due Jan 29, 2013: HW- Question #1 on Esther Ch. 1 Worksheet

Due Feb 11, 2013: HW- Esther Ch. 3:9-15

Finish reading chapter three of Megillat Esther.  You should be familiar with the sequence of events related in the chapter. 

Due Feb 21, 2013: HW- Read Ch. 8 of Megillat Esther

Due Mar 06, 2013: HW- Questions on Aishdas article (Kiddushin 30a)

Due Mar 13, 2013: Quiz (take-home)- Kiddushin 30a

Due Apr 12, 2013: HW- Translation of text on Kiddushin 30b

The text begins with ת"ר נאמר כבד את אביך ואת אמך and ends with  כאילו דרתי ביניהם וכבדוני.

Due Apr 18, 2013: Quiz- Key Terms (Kiddushin 29a-30b)

Due Apr 29, 2013: Exam- Kiddushin 30b and Key Terms to date

The Gemara text you are responsible for is from "להשיאו אשה מנלן" to "כאילו דרתי ביניהם וכבדוני." Be sure to know well all of the Rashi and Tosafot we covered, as well as the other commentaries we studied.

The Key Terms are listed on the handouts for each section of the Gemara we have covered. Note that they are often repeated (i.e. they appear on a handout even if they have previously appeared).

Extra Credit will be given for anyone who can identify the names, generations and locations of the Babyonian roshei yeshivot - see here.

Due Apr 30, 2013: HW- Tosafot (u'vat imo u'kra'ato) (Kidd. 31a)

Read תוספות's comment "ובאת אמו וקרעתו" on לא כמוד א. What is תוספות saying here, and what might have prompted them to comment? How does תוספות change our understanding of the story? Can you find relevance to our discussion on Beraitan Beach last week?

Due May 02, 2013: HW- Read Intro to Kohelet (Jewish Study Bible)

The packet can be found here. 

Due May 23, 2013: HW- Tosafot D"H "Rav Yehudah Omeir mi-shel ben"

 See email for instructions.

Due Jun 04, 2013: Project - Commentary of Rambam's Hil. Mamrim, Ch. 6

See the handout for details.
