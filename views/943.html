Nov 28th, 2011

rcm

A special school-wide project involving the study of a special selection of ethical and moral sayings from rabbinic literature called "Pirke Avot,".  PDF and audio files will be posted for both students and parents.

 

Syllabus

Assessment Policy

Classroom Guidelines

Due Sep 07, 2012: Review

Review the vocabulary from Thursday's lesson. Make sure you are able to identify the Rashi and Tosafot on the page.

Due Sep 11, 2012: Rashi Script

Review the letters of רש"י script. Look on the Links for a sheet that will help you review. Make a list of all the problematic letters you find.

Due Sep 13, 2012: Find the text of the Mishnah

In the following section of text, a משנה is quoted. Indicate which words are the quote of the משנה.

ותנן נמי זה בא בחביתו וזה בא בקורתו נשברה כדו של זה בקורתו של זה פטור פתח בחבית וסיים בכד

Due Sep 14, 2012: Review the flow of the text

A link has been added entitled "Gemara Brura Section 1" Review what each of the blocks of text are indicating from the FIRST page of this document. Try to make sure that you can identify a statement, or a challenge or an answer to a question. Identify the key words and any vocabulary that is necessary to understand this sugya.

Due Sep 28, 2012: Test

Test on the first two section of the גמרא.

Due Oct 30, 2012: TEST

Test on the Gemara we have covered since the last test until the middle of 28a of masechet Bava Kamma. Also responsible for the information about marriage in Judaism.

Due Nov 29, 2012: Quiz

There will be a quiz on Thursday, Nov 29 covering the text we study on Monday and Tuesday of this week.  Review questions from students on Wednesday.

Due Dec 11, 2012: Review

Review the section dealing with the case of the newly freed slave. If there is anything which you do not understand clearly, prepare specific questions.

Due Dec 18, 2012: QUIZ

Quiz on Tuesday about the various cases that are presented as a means to determine the ruling regarding: עביד איניש דינא לנפשיה
