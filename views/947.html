Syllabus

This is a rapid moving and intense course that prepares students for the AP BC Calculus course in 12th grade.  The first trimester focuses on pre-calculus topic necessary for BC including vectors, polar coordinates and polar graphs, the polar form of complex number and DeMoivreâs Theorem and pre-requisites of calculus.  The second trimester begins the study of calculus with limits, derivatives and applications of differentiation including related rates, graph sketching, optimization and LâHopitalâs rule.  Every topic is approached graphically, analytically, numerically and verbally and technology is integrated appropriately.

 

 

 

Assessment Policy

 1. Tests will be announced at least one week in advance. Quizzes may be given with or without advanced notice.

2. Tests and quizzes must be completed in pencil.

3. If you miss a test or quiz due to absence, you should be prepared to make it up on the day you return. If this is not possible, contact me to make other arrangements.

4. If you know that you will miss a test or quiz (for example, sports, early dismissal), you must inform me and take the test ahead of that time.

Classroom Guidelines

 Upper School Classroom procedures:

1. Each day you are required to bring:

Your binder with paper, pencils, homework, planner, calculator: TI 83 or 84 (plus) and your covered textbook.

2. Notebook

Your binder should include: Notes and Classwork, Homework, Tests and Quizzes

3. Homework

Homework should be completed IN PENCIL on a separate sheet of paper.

In the upper right hand corner the paper should be labeled with: Name, Date, Page and Problem Numbers

In case you are absent, you are responsible for the assignment.

Check the website to get the assignment or call your buddy to get the assignment.

You are responsible to try the assignment and to do as much as you can.

Your homework should reflect your own work, but you may discuss ideas and procedures with other students.

4. Classroom Behavior:

Appropriate behavior is always expected. This includes respect for your classmates, for me and for any substitute teacher.

5. Absence:

If you know that you will be missing a class, it is your responsibility to inform me so that you can hand in your homework.

6. Cheating:

Any form of cheating is NOT tolerated; school policy will be enforced.

7. Extra Help:

If you need extra help, I will be happy to work with you. Set up an appointment to meet with me at lunch or at break.
